About PSITE CL & Terms and Conditions
About PSITE Central Luzon

The Philippine Society of Information Technology Educators – Central Luzon (PSITE CL) is a professional organization dedicated to the advancement of Information Technology education in the Central Luzon region. It serves as a platform for IT educators to collaborate, share knowledge, and promote best practices in teaching and learning.

PSITE CL is committed to providing its members with access to professional development opportunities, networking events, workshops, seminars, and conferences that keep them updated on the latest trends in IT education.

  2. Membership is subject to approval by the PSITE CL administration. You will be notified via email upon approval.

Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173)

In compliance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173) of the Philippines, PSITE Central Luzon is committed to protecting your personal data. By registering, you consent to the collection, use, and processing of your personal information for the following purposes:

  • Reporting and analytics — for internal organizational reporting and planning purposes only.

Your personal data will not be sold, disclosed, or transferred to unauthorized third parties without your explicit consent, except as required by law.

You have the right to access, correct, and object to the processing of your personal data. For concerns, you may contact the PSITE CL Data Protection Officer.
