FAQs

We may have the answer you were looking for. Browse through our list of frequently asked questions below to know more.

  • Who can join PSITE? Any Filipino educators or academic institutions under the Computer Science, Information Technology, Information Systems, Multimedia Education, and related programs may join through their respective PSITE Regional Chapters.
  • How do I join PSITE? PSITE Membership is managed by each Regional Chapter. You may access their respective Membership Registration Form online.
  • What are the Membership Types? There are 3 types of PSITE Membership: Institutional, Individual, and Enterprise.
  • How much is the membership? Institutional: PHP 3,000.00, Individual: PHP 500.00, Enterprise: PHP 5,000.00.
  • How will I get a Membership Certificate? The Membership Certificate is issued by your Regional Chapter.
